Mega FAQ to answer your questions

What's automated vs. what needs my approval?

Everything generates automatically. Nothing publishes without you. Copy, creative, landing pages, campaign structures, and optimization recommendations are all generated by the platform. You approve before anything goes live. Final creative sign-off, page publishing, campaign launches, and major budget changes require your click.

Is this just ChatGPT with a wrapper?

ChatGPT produces text. Olympus builds, installs, and launches. No prompting. Connected to your tools. Trained on real campaign data.

How much is AI vs. human?

Platform is AI. Support is human. Strategy, judgment calls, and interpretation are us. In a Telegram channel. Same-day response.

Ownership & portability

Do I own everything?

Yes. 100%. Written into the MSLA. Campaigns, copy, creative, landing pages, data. Leave and take it all. Funnels export as HTML. Creatives as full-res PNG/JPG/MP4. Copy as DOC/CSV. Full data export of all leads and interactions.

Are my assets used to train models for competitors?

No. Your brand assets, copy, and content are isolated. We aggregate anonymized performance patterns, but your specific IP is never shared or used for other accounts.

What happens if I cancel?

7 days to export everything. Account archived for 30 days in case you need recovery. Then permanently removed. We assist with migration if needed.

Testing & optimization

How many campaigns can I test per week?

No hard limit. Budget is the practical constraint. With $4K/month you'd run 2-3 campaigns with 25-30 creatives each. More budget, more tests.

Does it prevent learning phase thrash?

Yes. Respects Meta's learning phase. No changes that reset learning unnecessarily. No more than once per 72 hours per ad set.

What signals drive optimization?

CPL, cost per booked call, show rate, start rate. You set guardrails: "never spend more than $X per lead" or "pause if CPL exceeds $Y for 72 hours." The platform monitors and recommends. You approve.

Can I import existing pixel and conversion history?

Yes. Your existing Meta pixel connects directly. Historical data informs initial targeting. No starting from scratch.

Can I generate N variants changing one variable at a time?

Yes. Systematic isolation is core to the testing methodology. Take a winner. Generate 10 variants changing only the headline. Or only the CTA. Or only the visual hook.

Creative & brand control

Can I enforce brand guidelines across all outputs?

Yes. Logo placement, colors, fonts, disclaimers. All enforced. Upload brand guidelines as PDFs. The system ingests and references them. Safe zones, color palette, font hierarchy, and required disclaimers applied automatically.

Can I upload an ad I like and have it recreated in my brand?

Yes. You provide the reference. We recreate it with your identity.

What formats are supported?

Static, carousel, stories, reels, thumbnails, infographics. 1:1, 4:5, 9:16. Long-form video is manual production at Sigma.

Can creatives be reused across funnels, website, email?

Yes. Shared asset library. Downloaded at full resolution. Used anywhere. You own them.

Data, security & attribution

From first eyeball to conversion, what tracks this?

Hyros for attribution. GHL for the pipeline. Both connected. Hyros tracks cross-device, cross-session journeys. GHL is where leads live. All sources (paid, organic, referral) flow into one dashboard.

Multi-touch attribution and duplicate deconfliction?

Hyros handles multi-touch. GHL merges duplicates.

What patient data is stored and where?

Lead data in GHL. Campaign data in Olympus. No clinical data or PHI touches the system. Architecture is built to keep PHI out entirely by design.

Role-based access, MFA, audit logs?

All three. Yes. Configure who sees what. All actions logged with timestamps. 72-hour breach notification commitment.

Who do I actually talk to?

The founders. In a Telegram channel. Not junior account managers. Not a ticket queue. Strategy calls included at Alpha/Sigma.
